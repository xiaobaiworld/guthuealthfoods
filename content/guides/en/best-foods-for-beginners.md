## Start with foods you can actually repeat

The best beginner foods for gut health are not rare or complicated. They are foods you can find easily, cook simply, and use in more than one meal. That makes healthy eating less dependent on motivation and more dependent on a grocery routine you can actually keep.

The foods below cover simple breakfasts, fruit, vegetables, grains, legumes, healthy fats, and easy protein options. Pick a few first, then add more variety after those first habits start to feel normal.

## A simple beginner formula

- One breakfast base: oats or yogurt.
- One fruit: apple or blueberries.
- One vegetable: spinach or broccoli.
- One staple: brown rice, lentils, or chickpeas.
- One healthy fat: avocado, almonds, or olive oil.

You do not need to buy everything at once. A shorter list that you actually use is better than a perfect list that sits untouched in the kitchen.
